# Weapons

Weapons is a main-line Warfare (Power) [technology](../glossary/technology.md).

Each tier of Weapons improves [ship fire rate](../glossary/combat.md) by 10 per minute.

| Level | Ship Fire Rate (per minute) |
|-------|---------------------------|
| none  | 20                        |
| I     | 30                        |
| II    | 40                        |
| III   | 50                        |
| IV    | 60                        |
| V     | 70                        |
