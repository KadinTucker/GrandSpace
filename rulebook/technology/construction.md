# Construction

Construction is a main-line Empire (Power) [technology](../glossary/technology.md).

Each tier of Construction reduces the cost of purchasing a new [building](../glossary/building.md) by 50 [money](../glossary/building.md).

| Level | Building Cost |
|-------|---------------|
| none  | 500           |
| I     | 450           |
| II    | 400           |
| III   | 350           |
| IV    | 300           |
| V     | 250           |
